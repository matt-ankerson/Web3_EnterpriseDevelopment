Farm Details

Sunny Braes offers diverse opportunities.

Currently set up for sheep and cattle grazing; this farm presents many versatile features. A dairy platform could be set up here with comparatively minimal capital investment. The land comprises productive and versatile soils

Residence

Constructed in the late 1980s, the homestead is a solid family home.

The main house features four bedrooms, two bathrooms and a double garage with internal access. It is fully insulated and heated by a log burner and underfloor heating throughout.
Adjacent to the house is an open garage suitable for three vehicles. Gardens surround the house with an orchard, vegetable garden and a bird aviary. The house has a broadband link by means of long distance wireless technology, provided by Yrless Ltd.

Existing Farm Systems

Currently this property supports 1200 Wairere Ewes and caters for an additional 500 dairy calves as grazers.

Over the warmer months this property grows a huge excess of feed, currently stored as silage and baleage. In addition to that, much of the land is put into winter crop in order to feed the winter grazers.

Location

The nearest urban center - Lawrence, is approximately 17 km from the farm gate.

Beyond Lawrence, Alexandra and Dunedin are of similar driving distance (1 hour). Balclutha is a (lesser) 40 minute drive from the farm gate. At just a 10 minute drive away, Tuapeka Mouth offers superb trout fishing and a punt service for crossing the Clutha River.
